### 🎂 BitDevs Uyo Turns 1!

This session was both a celebration and a deep technical dive. We marked our **1-year anniversary**, celebrated **Pizza Day**, and completed **Chapter 7 of Mastering Bitcoin**.

---

### Agenda

### <strong>1. Chapter 7: Merkle Root, MAST & Taproot</strong>
- [Mastering Bitcoin: Chapter 7](https://github.com/bitcoinbook/bitcoinbook/blob/develop/ch07_authorization_authentication.adoc)
  - **Merkle Root** — The data structure and its role in Bitcoin
  - **MAST** (Merkelized Abstract Syntax Trees) — Authorization and authentication for complex spend conditions
  - **P2C** (Pay to Contract) — The cryptographic primitive underlying Taproot
  - **Taproot** — How it combines MAST and P2C to enable two spend paths; advantages for complex treasury holdings

---

### 🌱 Community Growth Highlight

A highlight was hearing from **Praise Unite**, a community member building a **Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization application** that leverages Bitcoin's immutability — specifically signature verification, transaction hashes, and on-chain messaging — to verify and prove transfer of ownership for real-world assets like land, once government-certified. The Taproot session proved directly useful to his project.
